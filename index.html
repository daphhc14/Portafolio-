About me

I am a Product Designer and UX/UI designer graduated from Tecnológico de Monterrey with a special interest in Technology and User Experience Design. I have experience in the design of both physical and digital technology products and I am familiar with 3D modelling software such as Rhino, Blender and Spline. Also with graphic design software such as Photoshop and illustrator as well as UX/UI design software such as Figma.

Currently my goal is to continue developing in the area of UX/UI design since I believe that through user research and design thinking methodologies we can design more meaningful and useful products for our users. I firmly believe that design is a means to make a positive impact on people's lives, and that is what I like most about my profession.
